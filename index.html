Jesse Gronsbell

Welcome!

I'm a data scientist at Alphabet's Verily Life Sciences. Prior to joining Verily, I revieved my PhD in Biostatistics at Harvard University under the direction of Professor Tianxi Cai. I was also a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Biomedical Data Science at the Stanford School of Medicine with Professor Lu Tian.

Though I'm generally excited about all things statistics, my primary interest is in the development of methods for risk prediction with modern digital data sources such as electronic health records and mobile health data.

When I am not doing statistics, you'll likely find me running, cooking, or walking my adorable dog, Teddy.
