Jesse Gronsbell

Welcome to my site! I am a data scientist at Alphabet's Verily Life Sciences. Prior to joining Verily, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Biomedical Data Science at the Stanford School of Medicine with Professor Lu Tian. Before that, I spent five amazing years at Harvard earning my PhD in Biostatistics under the direction of Professor Tianxi Cai.

My primary statistical interest is in risk prediction with modern digital data sources such as electronic health records and mobile health data. When I am not doing statistics, you'll likely find me running, cooking, or walking my adorable dog, Teddy.
