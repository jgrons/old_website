Jessica Gronsbell

I'm a fifth year PhD candidate in the Department of Biostatistics at Harvard University where I am fortunate enough to work with Dr. Tianxi Cai. My thesis work focuses on the development of efficient machine learning methods for risk prediction in settings where outcome information, unlike feature information, is difficult to obtain.

I received my undergraduate degree in Applied Mathematics at the University of California at Berkeley. I love visiting my alma mater - especially during the freezing Boston winters. GO BEARS!
